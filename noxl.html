NoxiouS League (Call of Duty: Mobile)

Search & Destroy 5v5 multiplayer tournament

GAMEPLAY RULES

1. Use of restricted items during a game

  • Straight disqualification will be given if you are caught using an item from the banned list and the win will be automatically given to the opponent.

2. Abuses & Exploitation
  • Exploiting game or map bugs/glitches/flaws (“exploits”) for the purposes of gaining an unfair advantage is prohibited. Exploits that are not specifically named here are still prohibited but will be dealt with on a case-by-case basis. Tournaments Staff reserve the right to decide what will be deemed as an exploit or abuse.

3. Hacking
  • Hacking or otherwise modifying the intended behavior of the game client is prohibited.

4. Scripting
  • The use of scripts are prohibited.

5. Use of Macros
    The use of macros are prohibited.

6. Use of Third Party Softwares
  • The use of any third-party addons, hooks, programs or wrappers that interact with or alter the client, its appearance or behaviour for the purposes of gaining an unfair advantage is prohibited.

7. Impersonations
  • Impersonating an eligible player by attempting to alias as them, using their account or by any other method is prohibited and will result in the aliasing player and the player they are aliasing as being removed from the tournament.

8. In-game names
  • Each player must have his/her primary nickname or something very similar as his game name during all matches to be easily identifiable for referees, broadcasters, and viewers. What counts as similar is for the Gamer.LK Staff to decide. The only additional information allowed in a player’s in­game name is clan tags or sponsor names.

9. Collusion
  • Collusion, match fixing, bribing a referee or match official, or any other action or agreement to intentionally influence (or attempt to influence) the outcome of any match or Event.

Restricted Items

Weapons; NA45, SVD, Rinetti, Melee

Operator skills; Ballistic Shield (Impenetrable defense), Bull charge (Impenetrable defense), H.I.V.E, Shadow Blade, Transform Shield, K9ine Uint, TAK-5, Kinetic Ammor

Lethal & Tactical Utilities; Trip Mine, Thermite, Molotov Cocktail, Gas Grenade, Heartbeat Sensor, Flash drone, Storm ball, Decoy bomb

Perks; Persistence, Restock, Recon, Martyrdom

Weapon Perks; Akimbo

Score-streaks; UAV, Advanced UAV, HAWK X3, Care Package, Shield Turret, Stealth Chopper, VTOL, Swarm, Lightning Strike, Orbital Laser

Map Pool

Summit, Standoff, Raid, Take-Off, Firing Range, Hackney Yard, Crossfire
